Milestones

2024

The First Workshop

Pathea opens a 30 sqm workshop in BKK1 with three artisans and dream of slow, ethical fashion.

Our first standalone boutique open on Street 240, Phnom Penh. The space double as a live atelier

Artisan Training Program

Launch of the MAISON BAG Artisan Academy, training the next generation of Cambodian leather craftspeople.
